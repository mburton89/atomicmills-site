FAQ

What makes 321FIT different?

It's designed like a retro music player: workouts = playlists, exercises = tracks. Pixel art animations, chiptune sounds, and an ETA clock make fitness fun and efficient.

Is it free?

Completely free and ad-free on both Android and iOS. No in-app purchases.

What's new in the latest update?

Version 1.6 (Dec 4, 2025): Full support for foldable phones (Razr, Z Flip), optimized layouts for narrow/tall screens, new splash screen and logo.

Who made 321FIT?

Indie developer Matthew Burton, released in 2019.

Where can I give feedback?

Email the developer directly or leave a review on the store. Updates are ongoing!
