About Atomic Mills

Founded in 1939, Atomic Mills was born at the intersection of post-war optimism and experimental breakfast science.

Led by visionary Dean Lockhart, we pioneered nuclear-powered cereals such as Flappy Flakes™ and Nukey Nectar™, giving generations of citizens the radiant start to their day they didn’t know they needed.

Our mission? To energize the morning with science, sunshine, and a dash of mild paranoia.
