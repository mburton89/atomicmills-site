About Us

Founded in 1939, Atomic Mills was born at the intersection of post-war optimism and experimental breakfast science.

Led by visionary Dean Lockhart, we pioneered nuclear-powered cereals such as Flappy Flakes™ and Nukey Nectar™—the world’s first honey made from radioactive bees. By harnessing the raw power of nuclear energy, Nukey Nectar™ provides unmatched speed and strength to those bold enough to spread it on their toast.

Our mission? To energize the morning with science, sunshine, and a wholesome blast of innovation.
