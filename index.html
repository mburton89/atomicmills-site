About Us

Founded in 1940, in a universe where bears can fly, Atomic Mills took flight with one radical mission: win the war... with breakfast.

As Allied forces searched for an edge, a team of barely-certified scientists answered the call. Armed with a loose understanding of radiation and boundless enthusiasm, they pioneered nuclear-infused food products designed to supercharge soldiers with unmatched speed, strength, and spirit.

From the launch of Flappy Flakes Cereal to the sticky brilliance of Nukey Nectar (harvested from radioactive bees) Atomic Mills turned science into sustenance and breakfast into a launch pad.
