FAQ

Is it really inspired by a true story?

Believe it or not—yes! Bearplane takes loose inspiration from Wojtek, the real-life bear who served alongside Polish soldiers in World War II. Our version adds a few more jet engines and bazookas... but the heart of it is pure wartime legend.

What was the development of Bearplane like?

Bearplane took flight in 2020 during lockdown. With travel and work suddenly off the table, I found myself deep in the weird corners of history, where I discovered the incredible story of Wojtek. It was bizarre, funny, and strangely heroic… I HAD to make a game about it.

I was inspired by the tonal contrast in Fallout—dark, brutal combat wrapped in cheerful 1940s vibes. I wanted Bearplane to feel like that too: chaotic action in a cartoon world. I also borrowed from Skyrim’s shout mechanic and the physical comedy of Tom and Jerry, where the audio always perfectly matches the slapstick mayhem.

For two years, I focused entirely on getting the movement and feel just right, much like how Super Mario 64 was built around Mario’s movement before anything else.

By 2023, I brought on incredible artists—Leo Seibert, Jordan Wadel, and Vinnie Peters—who helped bring Bearplane to life. Since then, over 7,000 hours (and cups of coffee) have gone into turning this very reasonable idea of a bear with a jetpack into a fully playable game.

Is Bearplane single-player or multiplayer?

Both! You can fly solo or connect a controller for local co-op. Player 2 controls Rocket Rat and his trusty rifle. Team up and double the firepower!

What devices and controls are supported?

On mobile, play with touch controls or a gamepad. On PC, use mouse and keyboard or a controller. Bearplane also runs great on Steam Deck.

Does Bearplane support gamepads?

Yes! Gamepad support is built in across all platforms.

Can I play Bearplane offline?

Absolutely. No Wi-Fi? No problem. You can play offline with no penalties or restrictions.

Can I share my progress between devices?

Not yet, but we’re working on cloud sync for a future update so you can blast baddies seamlessly across all your devices.

Will there be future updates?

Definitely! We’re planning monthly updates packed with new hats, game modes, and surprises.

Is Bearplane kid-friendly?

Bearplane features cartoon violence, dismemberment, and blood—think Saturday morning mayhem with a slight edge. Parental discretion is advised.

Is the game available in other languages?

The full game is in English, but Rocket Rat’s voice lines and tutorials are localized into 8 additional languages: French, Spanish, German, Polish, Portuguese, Italian, Japanese, and Simplified Chinese.

Do hats affect gameplay?

They sure do! Each hat comes with a unique power or twist that changes how you play. It’s flair with firepower.

How do I unlock the full game on mobile?

Just tap the “Buy Full Game” button on the main menu to get all the goodies and unleash the full Bearplane experience.
